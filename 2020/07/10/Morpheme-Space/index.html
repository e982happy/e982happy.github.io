Morpheme Space

The focus of this experimental studio was to rethink architectural design approaches we learnt during the past years, and develop a new personalized approach. This case shows the attempt to collect the most basic elements of light and shadow, and then collage them into an integrated design.

During the final phase of this approach, composited buildings will stay flexible enough so that they can embed themselves into the topography and interact with the environment.
